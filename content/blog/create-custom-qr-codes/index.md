## Best Practices for QR Codes

*   **Contrast is King:** Ensure there's enough contrast between the foreground and background. Dark on light is safest.
*   **Size Matters:** If you're printing it, make sure it's at least 1x1 inch (2.5x2.5 cm).
*   **Test It:** Always scan your code with multiple phones before printing 1,000 flyers!
